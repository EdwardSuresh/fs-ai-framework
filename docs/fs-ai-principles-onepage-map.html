Row 1 — Trust & Accountability
01
Accountable Human Ownership

Every AI system has a named business owner and model owner. Accountability stays with humans — "the model did it" is not a defense.

02
Licensed Advice Boundary

No AI output becomes client advice until a licensed professional reviews and adopts it. AI cannot hold a license or owe a duty.

03
Explainability Proportional to Impact

Underwriting, pricing, claims, and credit decisions must be explainable — plainly to clients, technically to regulators. Where the law demands a reason, an unexplainable model is unusable.

04
Client Best Interest by Design

Best-interest and suitability live inside the optimization objective, not in post-hoc checks. An optimizer pursues exactly what it is told to maximize.

✦ The ReframeThe question that matters is not what AI can do — it is what the institution can stand behind.
Row 2 — Risk & Compliance
05
Model Risk Management for All AI

Every model — GenAI, agents, vendor-embedded AI — is inventoried, risk-tiered, and independently validated before and during use.

06
Regulatory Traceability

Every AI-influenced decision is reconstructable end-to-end: data, model and prompt versions, output, human action — retained per books-and-records.

07
Fairness Before & After Deployment

Underwriting, pricing, and claims models are tested for disparate impact and proxy discrimination pre-deployment — and monitored in production.

08
Compliance Gates Precede Capability

No AI reaches production without compliance, legal, and risk gates. Surveillance and supervision extend to AI-generated content and actions.

⚖ The GateCapability never precedes compliance.
Validated. Gated. Then live.

Row 3 — Data & Model Integrity
09
Data Sovereignty & Confidentiality

Client PII, policy data, and MNPI are processed only inside governed boundaries. No firm data feeds unapproved external models — and vendors never train on it.

10
Grounded, Approved Knowledge Only

Client-facing outputs are grounded in approved, current, citable sources. Ungrounded generation is never presented as fact — a hallucinated figure is a misrepresentation.

11
Model & Prompt Change Control

Models, prompts, and agents are versioned and promoted through change control. No silent change reaches production — including vendor model updates.

◈ The BoundaryData never leaves governance.
Grounded. Versioned. Citable.
Row 4 — Resilience & Market Integrity
12
Fail to Safe, Fail to Human

Failure or low confidence falls back to humans, manual process, or conservative defaults. Silent failure is prohibited — an outage must never become a conduct incident.

13
Market Integrity & Autonomy Limits

Trading AI operates inside hard limits — pre-trade controls, throttles, tested kill switches — enforced in a control layer outside the model's reach.

14
Continuous Monitoring & Drift Response

Production AI is watched for performance, drift, and anomalies — with thresholds that trigger review, restriction, retraining, or retirement.

⟳ The Operating TruthFailures are certain; incidents are optional.
The safe path must be the easy path.
